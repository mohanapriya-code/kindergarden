Programs We Offer

Pre-School

Our Pre-School program introduces young learners to structured routines and social interaction in a caring and playful setting. We focus on developing essential motor skills,stories, hands-on activities, and imaginative play.

Features
Sensory exploration and creative expression
Introduction to colors, shapes, and numbers
Social and emotional development through group play
Daily routines that build confidence and independence
Pre-Kindergarden

Our Pre-Kindergarten curriculum prepares children for kindergarten and beyond. Through guided learning and purposeful play, we introduce foundational academic concepts while nurturing curiosity and self-regulation.

Features
Pre-reading and early writing skills
Problem-solving through STEM-based activities
Emotional intelligence and teamwork
Teacher-led lessons and child-initiated projects
Enrichment-Activity

We offer a wide range of enrichment activities that support holistic development and ignite a love for lifelong learning.Each activity is designed to enhance creativity, coordination, and confidence

Features
Music & Movement
Arts & Crafts
Outdoor Exploration & Gardening
Mindfulness and Yoga Sessions
